#### Sea Elf Traits
<br>
**Ability Score Increase.** Your Constitution score increases by 1.

**Sea Elf Training.** You have proficiency with the spear, trident, light crossbow,
and net.

**Child of the Sea.** You have a swimming speed of 30 feet, and you can breathe air
and water.

**Friend of the Sea.** Using gestures and sounds, you can communicate simple ideas
with any beast that has an innate swimming speed.

**Languages.** You can speak, read, and write Aquan.
